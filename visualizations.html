Visual Story

Working with this dataset made me notice significant differences between region sales and the variable popularity of games and genres. Similarly, I wondered if there were any sales patterns lining up with particular region-based game publishers and the region they are sold in – how do games created by Japanese publishers perform in Japan and North America? How do games created by American publishers perform in North America and Japan?

Evidently, games produced by Japanese publishers tend to perform better in Japan than in North America – the blue area indicates the difference in sales amount. There is significantly more blue present for these game sales.

The sales of games created by American publishers, however, sell almost equally in Japan as well as North America! The areas line up almost exactly, with much less blue present.

Comparing these region-focused sales emphasizes the prominence of these particular American game companies internationally.
