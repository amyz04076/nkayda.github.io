Our Goal

Our goal is examine Metro-Vancouver over time at different scales to explore, create connections, and draw conclusions on how information such as population, income, and transit infrastructure are interconnected. We also seek to inform and entertain users, allowing them to explore snapshots of time in Metro-Vancouver’s history and learn about major events and developments.
