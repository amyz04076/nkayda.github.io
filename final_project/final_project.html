Introduction

The SkyTrain

Our goal is examine Metro-Vancouver over time at different scales to explore, create connections, and draw conclusions on how information such as population, income, and transit infrastructure are interconnected. We also seek to inform and entertain users, allowing them to explore snapshots of time in Metro-Vancouver’s history and learn about major events and developments.

Key development moments

It’s easy to notice bunches of stations popping up over time. Some notable development projects include:

Canada Line – 2009

15 stations were added as part of the Canada Line, adding 19.2 km to the SkyTrain network and a direct connection to Vancouver International Airport (YVR).

Millennium Line – 2002 to 2006

15 stations were added as part of the Canada Line, adding 19.2 km to the SkyTrain network and a direct connection to Vancouver International Airport (YVR).

Evergreen Expansion – 2016

The Millennium Line was extended by 6 stations, adding 11 km to the SkyTrain network deeper into Coquitlam.
